# /draft-content - Draft Marketing Content

Generate marketing content tailored to specific types, audiences, and brand voices.

## Usage

```
/draft-content <content type> <topic> [audience] [tone]
```

## Supported Content Types

- **Blog post**: Multi-section structure with engaging headlines, hook, and CTA
- **Social media**: Platform-specific formatting with hooks, hashtags, and engagement prompts
- **Email newsletter**: Subject lines with open-rate considerations, preview text, and CTAs
- **Landing page**: Hero copy, value propositions, social proof placement, and FAQs
- **Press release**: Standard format with dateline, lead paragraph, and quote placeholders
- **Case study**: Challenge-solution-results structure with metric requirements

## Workflow

### 1. Gather Requirements

Collect:
- Content type
- Topic and target audience
- Key messages (2-4 points)
- Tone and length specifications
- Brand voice guidelines (if configured)

### 2. Draft Content

Apply the content-creation skill templates for the specified type. Include SEO recommendations (primary keywords, meta descriptions, internal linking).

### 3. Review and Iterate

Offer revision options and suggest next steps (related content pieces, distribution plan).

## Tips

- Mention "interactive" for social media to get engagement-focused copy
- Specify the funnel stage to get appropriately targeted messaging
- Request multiple variations for A/B testing
