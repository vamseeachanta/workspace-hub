# /campaign-plan - Generate Campaign Brief

Generate a comprehensive marketing campaign brief with objectives, audience, messaging, channel strategy, content calendar, and success metrics.

## Usage

```
/campaign-plan <campaign goal or description>
```

## Inputs

1. **Campaign goal** -- the primary objective (e.g., drive signups, increase awareness, launch a product, generate leads)
2. **Target audience** -- who the campaign is aimed at
3. **Timeline** -- campaign duration and any fixed dates
4. **Budget range** -- approximate budget (optional)
5. **Additional context** (optional): product details, differentiators, previous learnings, brand guidelines, geographic focus

## Campaign Brief Structure

### 1. Campaign Overview
- Campaign name suggestion
- One-sentence summary
- Primary objective with specific, measurable goal

### 2. Target Audience
- Primary and secondary audience segments
- Pain points and motivations
- Buying stage alignment

### 3. Key Messages
- Core campaign message (one sentence)
- 3-4 supporting messages
- Proof points for each message

### 4. Channel Strategy
For each recommended channel: why it fits, content format recommendations, effort level, budget allocation

### 5. Content Calendar
Week-by-week content plan with channel, owner, and dependencies

### 6. Content Pieces Needed
All required assets with type, description, priority, and timeline

### 7. Success Metrics
Primary KPI with target, secondary KPIs, tracking method, reporting cadence

### 8. Budget Allocation (if budget provided)

### 9. Risks and Mitigations

### 10. Next Steps

## Follow-Up Options

- Dive deeper into any section
- Draft specific content pieces from the calendar
- Create a competitive analysis to inform messaging
- Adjust for different budget or timeline
