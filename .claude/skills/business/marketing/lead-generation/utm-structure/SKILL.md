# UTM Structure

## UTM Structure


Source: Where traffic came from
Medium: Marketing medium
Campaign: Specific campaign
Content: A/B test variant
Term: Keywords (for paid search)

Example:
?utm_source=linkedin
&utm_medium=social
&utm_campaign=fatigue-calculator
&utm_content=engineer-post
```
