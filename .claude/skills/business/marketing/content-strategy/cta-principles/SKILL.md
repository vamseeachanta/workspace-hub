# CTA Principles

## CTA Principles


- Use action verbs: "Get", "Start", "Download", "Join", "Try", "See"
- Be specific about what happens next
- Create urgency when genuine
- Reduce risk: "No credit card required", "Cancel anytime"
- One primary CTA per page or email
