# Best Practices

## Best Practices


### Do


1. Maintain consistent posting schedule
2. Engage with followers and industry peers
3. Use platform-native content formats
4. Track and analyze metrics weekly
5. Repurpose content across platforms
6. Respond to comments/mentions promptly

### Don't


1. Cross-post identical content everywhere
2. Ignore negative feedback
3. Over-promote services
4. Post without strategy
5. Buy followers or engagement
6. Neglect analytics
