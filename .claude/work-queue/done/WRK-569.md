## What

Map the 5,496 OrcaFlex `.dat` model files (dde/Orcaflex) into the
`specs/data-sources/` YAMLs for `digitalmodel`, `saipem`, and `rock-oil-field`
as `orcaflex_models:` entries.

## Mapping Plan

```yaml

```
