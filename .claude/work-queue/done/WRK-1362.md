## Mission

Calculate parachute drag force for all applicable load cases including single and dual chute configurations per Stroud sizing charts.

## What

Existing `parachute_drag.py` calculates drag for a single chute. Per the Stroud sizing data in WRK-5082:
- **Single chute**: speeds up to ~200 MPH (model 430 Std. 32 for 3200-4000 lbs)
- **Dual chutes**: speeds over 200 MPH (models 430-28/430-30/450/470 for 2800-4000 lbs)

Need to assess both configurations and determine which applies at each speed.

## Why

GT1R R35 at 3,600 lbs:
- At 200 MPH: single chute may be adequate (Stroud 430 Std. 32)
- At 250 MPH: dual chutes required per Stroud chart (430-28 or 430-30 Pro-Mod range)
- Frame must be designed for the governing load case
