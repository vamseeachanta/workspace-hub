## Mission

Write `scripts/knowledge/synthesize-archive.py` with TDD.

Parses all 409 archived WRKs, backfills knowledge base, generates synthesis report.

### Inputs per WRK
- `archive/YYYY-MM/WRK-NNN.md` — frontmatter
- `assets/WRK-NNN/evidence/future-work.yaml` — follow-ons
- `assets/WRK-NNN/evidence/resource-intelligence.yaml` — gaps
- `assets/WRK-NNN/evidence/cost-summary.yaml` — cost

- `knowledge-base/wrk-completions.jsonl` — backfilled (21 to 409)
- `docs/archive-synthesis-report.yaml` — grouped by category
- Cross-WRK analysis: category heat map, repeat spawners, cost-vs-value
