## Mission

Convert the 15 deterministic gate rules currently enforced only by LLM prose into
verifiable scripts/hooks. Non-deterministic rules (N1–N5) documented in stage contracts
as "LLM judgment required." Eliminates silent gate breaches from context rot.

## Why

- Scripts/code are superior to LLM judgment for deterministic checks (patterns.md hard rule)
- Context rot causes LLM to forget prose rules mid-session — scripts never forget
- 3-agent sim showed 95% compliance with prose alone; D-items bring it to 100% mechanical
