## Description

Review and research the [get-shit-done](https://github.com/gsd-build/get-shit-done) repo to identify project management features that could enhance the `/work` queue system. Evaluate against our current working style and skill ecosystem.

## Scope

1. Clone/fetch and analyze the get-shit-done repository structure, features, and patterns
2. Map its project management capabilities against our current `/work` queue system
3. Identify gaps and enhancement opportunities (prioritization, dependencies, scheduling, reporting)
4. Propose actionable enhancements that fit our WRK lifecycle and skill architecture
5. Document findings as a research brief
