# Garage clean up

## What
Clean up and organize the garage space. Remove unnecessary items, sweep and tidy the area, and arrange remaining items for easy access.

## Why
Maintain a clean and functional garage space for storage and vehicle use.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Unnecessary items removed or disposed of
- [ ] Garage swept and tidied
- [ ] Remaining items organized and accessible
