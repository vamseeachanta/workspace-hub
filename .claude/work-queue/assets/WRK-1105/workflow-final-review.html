Part 1 — Diagnosis: why does work-done go into MEMORY.md?

  • Audit the current flow: when a WRK is archived, where do learnings go and why?
  • Identify: comprehensive-learning.sh / Stage 15 future-work / Stage 16 resource-intelligence-update — what do they actually write and where?
  • Diagnose: why is the "WRK-NNN ARCHIVED" summary line landing in MEMORY.md instead of a structured knowledge artifact?
  • Map: what is resource-intelligence's actual role vs what it should be doing for post-work knowledge capture

Part 2 — Architecture: right destination for each knowledge type

Define a clear routing table: | Knowledge type | Current destination | Correct destination | |---------------|--------------------|--------------------| | WRK completion summary | MEMORY.md (wrong) | ? | | Reusable patterns from work | MEMORY.md (partial) | ? | | Script/tool inventory | MEMORY.md (partial) | resource-intelligence skill? | | Career learnings / domain expertise | nowhere | ? | | Cross-repo architectural decisions | MEMORY.md (partial) | ? | | Engineering domain knowledge (FEA, CFD, OrcaFlex, etc.) | nowhere | ? |

Part 3 — Implementation: structured knowledge system

  • scripts/knowledge/ — scripts to capture, index, and surface knowledge
  • A knowledge-base/ or equivalent persistent store accessible to all sessions
  • Integration with resource-intelligence Stage 2 — enrich planning with past learnings
  • Integration with session-start — surface relevant knowledge at session open
  • Career learnings ingestion — structured way to encode domain expertise (engineering, finance, software) that persists and is queryable

Part 4 — Migration

  • Move WRK completion summaries OUT of MEMORY.md into proper knowledge artifacts
  • MEMORY.md should stay ≤200 lines of pointers and active state only

MEMORY.md is being used as a catch-all for everything, growing toward its 200-line limit. The real problem is there is no structured, queryable, long-lived knowledge store. Work done in 2024 or 2025 is inaccessible to the current session. Career-level engineering expertise (OrcaFlex, FEA, pipeline integrity, energy economics) exists nowhere in the system. Each session rediscovers patterns that were already learned.

Resource-intelligence (Stage 2) is supposed to bring past learnings INTO planning — but it's only scanning docs/skills, not a real knowledge base. This WRK fixes that gap end-to-end.
