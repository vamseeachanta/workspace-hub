Survey the following source families and extract key formulae, assumptions, and applicability limits:

  1. Actuator-disk / momentum theory — axial and tangential slipstream velocity at rudder plane; Rankine-Froude relationships
  2. Holtrop & Mennen (1982, 1984) — semi-empirical resistance and propulsion corrections including rudder interaction factors
  3. Söding (1998) / Brix (1993) "Manoeuvring Technical Manual" — practical rudder-in-propeller-slipstream formulations
  4. Molland & Turnock — "Marine Rudders and Control Surfaces" (2007); systematic experimental and RANS data
  5. ITTC procedures — relevant maneuvering and propulsion test procedures that define interaction coefficients
  6. Recent RANS/CFD studies (2010–2024) — validation data and correction factors for semi-empirical methods

The implementation quality depends entirely on selecting a well-validated method. A thorough literature base prevents re-deriving known results and ensures the chosen approach is benchmarked.
