Status

Prompt Start Context

Collect photos from three source devices (Sabitha's phone, Vamsee's phone, and Doris's computer) and upload them to the achantas-media repository. Photos should be organized by date and source during the process.

Why

Centralize family photo collection in achantas-media for unified access, backup, and sharing.

Acceptance Criteria

  • [ ] Photos collected from Sabitha's phone
  • [ ] Photos collected from Vamsee's phone
  • [ ] Photos collected from Doris's computer
  • [ ] Photos organized by date and source
  • [ ] All photos uploaded to achantas-media

Plan

  • Step 1: Collect photos from each device (AirDrop/USB for phones, network share for computer)
  • Step 2: Organize by EXIF date and source device into YYYY/MM/ directory structure. For photos without EXIF date metadata, fall back to (a) filename date pattern parsing (e.g., IMG_20250115_*), then (b) file modification timestamp, then (c) place in YYYY/MM/unsorted/ using the file's filesystem date. Store photos in original format (HEIC, JPEG, PNG) without conversion; deduplication operates on original files only
  • Step 3: Deduplicate using hash comparison (SHA-256)
  • Step 3.5: Verify achantas-media repo supports large binary storage (Git LFS or similar). If total photo collection exceeds 1GB, configure LFS tracking for image extensions before committing
  • Step 4: Upload organized collection to achantas-media repo
  • Step 5: Verify upload by comparing: (a) total source count across all devices, (b) duplicates removed count, (c) final destination count. Assert that source_total - duplicates_removed = destination_count
