The Well — acoustic_scattering datasets for subsea NDE validation
Status

Prompt Start Context

Integrate The Well's acoustic_scattering datasets (3 variants: basic, maze, multi-scatter) into digitalmodel as benchmark data for subsea Non-Destructive Evaluation (NDE) and inspection analysis. These datasets simulate acoustic wave scattering around obstacles — directly relevant to ultrasonic inspection of subsea structures.

The Well package: pip install the_well (v1.2.0, CC BY 4.0) Datasets: acoustic_scattering, acoustic_scattering_maze, acoustic_scattering_inclusions

Why

WRK-393 identified acoustic scattering as top-3 integration candidate. Subsea inspection and NDE uses ultrasonic/acoustic methods to detect corrosion, cracks, and wall loss. Having physics-accurate acoustic scattering simulation data enables: - Validating digitalmodel NDE analysis modules against ground-truth physics - Training defect detection models with synthetic acoustic data - Benchmarking acoustic signal processing algorithms
