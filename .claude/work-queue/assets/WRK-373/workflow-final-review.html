Prompt Start Context

Produce a strategic vision document for the ACE Engineering ecosystem of repositories that bridges where we are today to the "Agile Adaptive Autonomous Production" future described in Zvi Feuer's LinkedIn article. The deliverable is a single authoritative docs/vision/VISION.md in workspace-hub that:

  1. Articulates the current mission of each major repo in plain language
  2. Maps each repo's trajectory toward autonomous, adaptive engineering workflows
  3. Identifies the capability gaps between today and the autonomous-production future
  4. Proposes a 3-horizon roadmap (horizon 1 = now, 2 = 6–18 months, 3 = 18 months+)

Extensive online research is required to: - Read and synthesise the source article and its cited concepts (Industry 4.0, Digital Twin, AI-driven operations, adaptive manufacturing) - Survey comparable open-source and commercial engineering platforms taking the same journey - Ground the vision in real industry direction, not just internal aspiration

Why

The workspace-hub repositories span computational engineering (OrcaFlex, OpenFOAM, FEA), digital asset management, data pipelines, and AI orchestration. Without an explicit shared vision, each repo optimises locally. A unified future-state vision lets every WRK item be evaluated against a north star: does this move us toward autonomous adaptive production?

This also serves as a reference for onboarding new contributors and for marketing via aceengineer-website.

Acceptance Criteria

  • [ ] Source article fully read and key concepts extracted (Industry 4.0, Digital Twin, AAAP loop, etc.)
  • [ ] Secondary research complete — at least 3 corroborating industry sources surveyed
  • [ ] Current-mission summary written for each major repo (digitalmodel, assetutilities, assethold, worldenergydata, aceengineer-website, workspace-hub itself)
  • [ ] Capability-gap analysis table completed (current state vs autonomous-production future)
  • [ ] 3-horizon roadmap drafted with concrete capability milestones per horizon
  • [ ] WRK-item candidates identified for horizon 1 and 2 gaps
  • [ ] docs/vision/VISION.md created in workspace-hub and committed
  • [ ] Vision reviewed and approved by user before commit
