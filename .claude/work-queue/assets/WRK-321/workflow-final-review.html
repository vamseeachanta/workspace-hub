Prompt Start Context

Extend field development economics model to include MIRR/NPV with carbon cost sensitivity. Carbon price scenarios ($/tonne CO2), Scope 1/2 emissions intensity input, breakeven carbon price output. Feed from existing decline curve forecasts (WRK-318).

Why

Carbon pricing is increasingly material to field development economics. Adding carbon cost sensitivity to NPV/MIRR analysis enables screening of development scenarios against carbon price trajectories, supporting investment decisions that account for energy transition risk.

Acceptance Criteria

  • [x] NPV calculation with configurable discount rate and cash flow schedule
  • [x] MIRR (Modified Internal Rate of Return) calculation
  • [x] Carbon cost input: $/tonne CO2, Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions intensity (tCO2/boe)
  • [x] Sensitivity analysis: NPV vs. carbon price curve (tornado chart output)
  • [x] Breakeven carbon price calculation (NPV = 0 solve)
  • [x] Integration with WRK-318 decline curve forecasts for production profile input
  • [x] Pytest coverage with known economics examples (35 tests, all passing)
