## Mission

Make `whats-next.sh` exclude work items whose `not_before` date is in the future, so deferred items don't appear in the "run now" list.

## What

`scripts/work-queue/whats-next.sh` reads the `not_before` field but only renders it as a display note. Items with `not_before` in the future (e.g. WRK-1109 deferred to 2026-04-10) still show up in the recommended work list, which is misleading.

## Why

The whole point of `not_before` is to defer items. Showing them in `/whats-next` output defeats that purpose and causes users to attempt running deferred work.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Items with `not_before > today` are excluded from all recommendation buckets (UNCLAIMED_ACTIVE, HIGH_UNBLOCKED, MED_UNBLOCKED, etc.)
- [ ] Items with `not_before <= today` or no `not_before` field are unaffected
- [ ] A "Deferred" section or count is optionally shown so users know items exist but are hidden
- [ ] Existing tests (if any) updated; manual smoke test with a future-dated item confirms filtering
