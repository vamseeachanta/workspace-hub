## Mission

Create a high-level 3D schematic showing the full problem context: GT-R car body, parachute mounting frame, deployed parachute canopy, and 50 ft lanyard.

## What

- Simplified car body (R35 GT-R proportions)
- Parachute frame structure (from frame_geometry_3d.py)
- Deployed parachute canopy (hemispherical, 12 ft diameter)
- 50 ft lanyard from frame bracket to canopy
- Force arrows (drag direction)
- Key dimensions annotated

Communicates the engineering problem at a glance for client review and engineering report (WRK-1368).
