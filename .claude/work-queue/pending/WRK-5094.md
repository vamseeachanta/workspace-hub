## Mission

Create a reusable real estate tax skill for the workspace-hub ecosystem that automates and standardizes CRE (commercial real estate) tax preparation across properties and tax years.

## What

Build `.claude/skills/personal-finance/real-estate-tax/SKILL.md` covering:

### Core Capabilities
1. **IRC §164(d) property tax allocation** — auto-compute buyer/seller split based on closing date and tax year; adjust cost basis for seller's share
2. **MACRS depreciation schedules** — 39-year nonresidential, mid-month convention; support for cost segregation reclassification (5/7/15/39-year components with bonus depreciation rates by year)
3. **Cost basis computation** — purchase price + capitalizable closing costs + IRC §164(d) adjustment; land/building split from HCAD appraisal
4. **NNN lease accounting** — track tenant reimbursable expenses (taxes, insurance, HOA), generate reimbursement request letters, compute 180-day documentation deadlines
5. **Tax scenario modeling** — compare standard vs cost-seg depreciation, cash vs accrual, C-Corp vs S-Corp pass-through
6. **Form preparation checklist** — Form 1120, Form 8825, Form 4562, TX No Tax Due + PIR, Form 7004 (extension)

### Scripts
- `scripts/tax/compute-depreciation.py` — given basis, placed-in-service date, asset class → annual depreciation schedule (YAML output)
- `scripts/tax/compute-164d-allocation.py` — given closing date, total taxes, seller credit → buyer deductible + basis adjustment
- `scripts/tax/generate-reimbursement-request.py` — given property, tax year, paid bills → tenant reimbursement letter (markdown)
- `scripts/tax/tax-worksheet-validator.py` — validate tax-preparation-worksheet.yaml against schema

### Templates
- `config/tax/property-tax-worksheet-template.yaml` — schema for per-property tax worksheets
- `config/tax/depreciation-schedule-template.yaml` — multi-year depreciation tracker

### Knowledge Codified from WRK-5093
- HCAD land/building ratio method for allocation
- 12-month rule for prepaid insurance (cash basis)
- TCJA bonus depreciation phase-down schedule (2022: 100%, 2023: 80%, 2024: 60%, 2025: 40%, 2026: 20%, 2027+: 0%)
- TX franchise tax No Tax Due threshold ($2.47M for 2025)
- Harris County / Alief ISD / MUD tax payment portals and deadlines
- Family Dollar NNN lease reimbursement terms and 180-day documentation rule
- C-Corp vs S-Corp election deadlines and late relief procedures

## Why

WRK-5093 tax preparation required manually reading 15+ documents and computing depreciation, IRC §164(d) splits, and scenario comparisons by hand. With multiple properties (current + future acquisitions), this work recurs annually. Per the 25% repetition rule, every repeatable operation must become a script. This skill makes tax season a structured pipeline rather than ad-hoc document archaeology.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. SKILL.md created with trigger, command interface, and integration points
2. `compute-depreciation.py` produces correct MACRS schedule (verified against IRS Publication 946 tables)
3. `compute-164d-allocation.py` matches manual WRK-5093 computation
4. Tax worksheet schema validates the existing `tax-preparation-worksheet.yaml`
5. Skill integrates with work-queue (auto-creates annual tax WRK items per entity)
