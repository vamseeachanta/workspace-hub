## Mission

Create automated regulatory compliance checking against extracted requirements from SOLAS 2020, IMO A.749, ABS Rules, and DNV standards. Leverage the 3,724 extracted requirement records.

## What

1. Parse extracted requirements into machine-checkable rules
2. IMO intact stability criteria (6 checks)
3. SOLAS subdivision and damage stability
4. ABS structural requirements (minimum scantlings)
5. DNV environmental load requirements
6. Compliance report generation (pass/fail/margin per criterion)

## Reference Sources

- SOLAS 2020 Consolidated Edition (manifest exists)
- UK MCA MSIS43 (manifest exists)
- ABS Intro to Rules and Guides (manifest exists)
- 3,724 requirements in requirements.jsonl

## Acceptance Criteria

1. ≥ 10 automated compliance checks implemented
2. Report generation with pass/fail and margin
3. Tests against known vessel compliance data
