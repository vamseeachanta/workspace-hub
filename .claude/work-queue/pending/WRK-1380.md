## Mission

Fill in the ship-dimensions.yaml template (generated by WRK-1339 Child E) with actual vessel dimensions from the 110 SNAME ship plan PDFs. This is manual work — plans are drawing-only with no extractable text.

## What

1. Run `generate-ship-dimension-template.py` to create initial template
2. Open each ship plan PDF and extract: LOA, beam, draft, depth, displacement, speed
3. Prioritize: capital ships (BB, CV, CA) first, then destroyers (DD), then auxiliaries
4. Cross-reference with Jane's Fighting Ships 2009-2010 (manifest exists) for verification
5. Mark `entry_status: verified` when cross-referenced

## Acceptance Criteria

1. ≥ 30 vessels with complete dimensions (Phase 1)
2. All capital ships (BB, CV, CA, CB) filled in
3. At least 5 cross-referenced against Jane's
4. ship-dimensions.yaml updated and committed
