## Problem

`whats-next.sh` shows feature WRKs with `status: coordinating` under `▶ WORKING`
even when they have no active stage and their children are pending/not started.
This makes the WORKING section misleading.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Coordinating feature WRKs are NOT shown under `▶ WORKING`
- [ ] A dedicated section (e.g. `◈ COORDINATING`) shows feature WRKs with child progress
  summary: `children: 3 total, 1 archived, 2 pending`
- [ ] A feature WRK with all children archived is promoted to `done` automatically
  (or flagged for manual close)
- [ ] Existing tests pass; add at least one test for the new section
