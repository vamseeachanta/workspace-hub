## Mission

Research, assess, and implement calculation frameworks for mooring system analysis — covering catenary mechanics, quasi-static and dynamic analysis, line sizing, and fatigue — suitable for fast-running engineering assessment.

## What / Why

Mooring systems keep floating structures on station. Accurate mooring analysis requires validated methods across multiple sub-domains:

- **Catenary mechanics** — elastic and inextensible catenary equations, touchdown point, line geometry
- **Quasi-static analysis** — vessel offset vs restoring force, environmental loading (API RP 2SK)
- **Dynamic analysis** — low-frequency and wave-frequency vessel motions, line dynamics
- **Line component selection** — chain, wire rope, polyester; breaking strength, weight, stiffness
- **Fatigue** — tension cycling, OPB/IPB at fairlead, T-N curves (API RP 2SK, DNV-OS-E301)
- **Installation** — pre-tension, anchor holding capacity, proof loading

Existing `digitalmodel/src/digitalmodel/subsea/mooring_analysis/` has catenary, designer, and OrcaFlex generator modules — this study will ground them in literature and fill gaps.
