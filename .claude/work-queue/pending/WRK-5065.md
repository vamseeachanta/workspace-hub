## Mission

Research, assess, and implement calculation frameworks for offshore structural analysis — covering tubular joint design, fatigue (S-N and fracture mechanics), member capacity, plate buckling, and jacket/topside assessment — suitable for fast-running engineering assessment.

## What / Why

Offshore structural analysis spans multiple disciplines. Existing `digitalmodel/src/digitalmodel/structural/` has modules for fatigue, pipe/plate capacity, jacket/topside, stress analysis, and offshore resilience. This study will:

- **Tubular joint design** — SCF parametric equations (Efthymiou, Lloyd's), joint classification, punching shear
- **Fatigue assessment** — S-N curves (DNV-RP-C203), cumulative damage (Miner's rule), thickness correction, environment factors
- **Member capacity** — tubular beam-column (API RP 2A, ISO 19902), interaction ratios, effective length
- **Plate/shell buckling** — DNV-RP-C201, Euler buckling, plate effective width, stiffened panel capacity
- **Connection design** — grouted connections, mechanical connectors, weld sizing
- **Jacket assessment** — pushover analysis concepts, reserve strength ratio (RSR), system reliability

Ground existing implementations in standards and extend coverage.
