## Mission

Systematically extract all worked examples, equations, tables, and numerical test data from the full SNAME naval architecture collection (~30 textbooks/references, 146 manifests). Produce clean input/output pairs suitable for TDD in digitalmodel.

## What

Current state: 1,691 records extracted (107 worked examples, 815 equations, 248 definitions, 521 procedures) from 30 unique documents. Extraction is shallow — many textbooks have dozens of worked problems that were missed, and OCR artifacts corrupt equation formatting.

Deepen extraction across all documents with focus on:

1. **Stability & hydrostatics** (Biran 1st/2nd ed, PNA Vol I, EN400 Ch 3-4): GZ curves, metacentric height, trim calculations, free surface effect, inclining experiments
2. **Resistance & propulsion** (PNA Vol II, Bertram, SNAME Ship Resistance & Flow): Holtrop-Mennen method parameters, model test data, propeller design charts, Taylor series
3. **Structural analysis** (Hughes & Paik, Eyres): hull girder bending, buckling, fatigue S-N curves, section modulus calculations
4. **Seakeeping & motions** (PNA Vol III, Newman, Journée): RAO data, strip theory examples, added mass coefficients, roll damping
5. **Offshore engineering** (Chakrabarti, DNV-RP-C205/H103): wave load calculations, Morison equation examples, slamming, VIV
6. **EN400 remaining** — chapters 5 (materials), 6 (structure), 9 (maneuverability) have worked examples not yet captured
7. **Ship plans dimensional data** — extract LOA, beam, draft, displacement from the 110 ship plan manifests

## Why

- Worked examples from textbooks are the highest-confidence TDD test data (peer-reviewed, published answers)
- digitalmodel needs verified test cases for hydrostatics, stability, resistance, and structural modules
- Current extraction captures <30% of available worked examples across the collection
- Clean numerical data enables regression testing as modules evolve

## Acceptance Criteria

1. All worked examples from all 30 documents extracted with clean given/find/answer separation
2. Equations extracted with proper LaTeX or structured notation (not garbled OCR)
3. Ship plans dimensional index populated (LOA, beam, draft, displacement for each vessel)
4. At least 300 worked examples total (up from current 107)
5. Each extraction record has `use_as_test: true/false` flag indicating TDD suitability
6. Extraction quality audit: <5% records with OCR artifacts in numerical values
