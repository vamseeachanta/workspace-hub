## Mission

Research, assess, and implement calculation frameworks for production engineering — covering vertical lift performance (VLP), inflow performance relationships (IPR), nodal analysis, multiphase flow correlations, and well performance optimisation.

## What / Why

Production engineering calculations predict well deliverability and optimise production. Existing `digitalmodel/src/digitalmodel/production_engineering/` has VLP correlations, IPR models, nodal solver, and quality scoring modules. This study will:

- **VLP correlations** — Beggs & Brill, Hagedorn-Brown, Duns-Ros, Orkiszewski, Gray; pressure drop in tubing
- **IPR models** — Vogel, Fetkovich, Jones-Blount-Glaze; reservoir inflow vs bottomhole pressure
- **Nodal analysis** — intersection of IPR and VLP at operating point; sensitivity analysis
- **Multiphase flow** — flow regime maps (Taitel-Dukler, Barnea), liquid holdup, pressure gradient
- **Gas lift** — optimisation, stability, valve mechanics, unloading design
- **ESP/artificial lift** — pump performance, system curves, variable speed drive

Ground existing VLP/IPR implementations in correlations from the original papers and validate.
