# Create Achantas Family Tree

## What
Build a family tree application/data structure for the Achantas family that connects and relates all family members, showing relationships (parent-child, spouse, sibling) across generations.

## Why
Preserve family history and relationships in a structured, browsable format that makes it easy to understand how all family members are connected.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Family member data model with key fields (name, DOB, relationships, generation)
- [ ] Relationship types supported: parent-child, spouse, sibling
- [ ] Visual or navigable family tree output
- [ ] Data stored in structured format (YAML/JSON) for easy editing
- [ ] All known family members included and connected
