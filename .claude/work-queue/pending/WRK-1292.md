## What

The 10 auto-generated research briefs from WRK-1181 have empty `key_equations: []` and `worked_examples: []` fields. The existing manually-curated `geotechnical-pile-capacity.yaml` shows the target quality — equations with LaTeX, worked examples with inputs/expected_output, and `use_as_test: true` flags for TDD.

With 202 PDFs now downloaded across 10 domains, agents can extract key equations and worked examples from the literature.

## Why

Worked examples with known answers are the highest-value output of the research skill — they feed directly into TDD test assertions. Without them, calculation implementations must derive test data from scratch.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Each Tier 1 domain brief has at least 3 `key_equations` with LaTeX
2. Each Tier 1 domain brief has at least 2 `worked_examples` with `use_as_test: true`
3. Worked examples include specific numeric inputs and expected outputs (not null)
4. Equations reference specific standard sections
