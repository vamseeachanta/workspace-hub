## Mission

Research, assess, and implement a comprehensive literature base for cathodic protection — covering sacrificial anode and impressed current design, coating breakdown, anode life calculation, and monitoring — synthesising the theoretical foundation behind existing CP modules.

## What / Why

Existing `digitalmodel/src/digitalmodel/cathodic_protection/` has 4 standard-specific modules (API RP 1632, DNV-RP-B401, ISO 15589-2, fuel system CP). WRK-1113 performed document-verified testing against project documents. This study complements that by building the **theoretical literature base** — the textbook and standards foundation that explains _why_ the methods work:

- **Electrochemistry fundamentals** — corrosion cell, Nernst equation, polarisation curves, Tafel kinetics
- **Sacrificial anode design** — anode material properties (Al, Zn, Mg), driving voltage, current output, anode life
- **Impressed current design** — system sizing, power supply, anode bed configuration, current distribution
- **Coating breakdown** — mean coating breakdown factor, DNV-RP-B401 Table 10-4, time-dependent degradation
- **Current demand** — bare vs coated surfaces, temperature effects, seawater resistivity
- **CP monitoring** — reference electrodes, potential surveys, criteria (−850 mV CSE, −800 mV Ag/AgCl)
