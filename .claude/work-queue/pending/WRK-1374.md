## Mission

Extend stability module beyond basic GZ curves to damage stability, compartmentalization analysis, and automated IMO/ABS intact stability criteria checking.

## What

1. **Intact stability criteria** (IMO A.749): GZ area checks, initial GM, weather criterion
2. **Damage stability**: lost buoyancy method, added weight method, floodable length
3. **Free surface effect**: multi-tank, non-rectangular tanks
4. **Parametric rolling risk**: Froude-Krylov restoring variation assessment
5. **Grain shifting moments** (for bulk carriers)

## Reference Sources

- Biran Ship Hydrostatics and Stability (1st/2nd ed) — full treatment
- SNAME PNA Vol I — damage stability methods
- UK MCA MSIS43 — intact stability guidance
- SOLAS 2020 — probabilistic damage stability
- EN400 Ch 4 — 5 worked examples

## Acceptance Criteria

1. IMO intact stability criteria checker (pass/fail with margin report)
2. Lost buoyancy damage calculation
3. TDD from Biran and EN400 worked examples
4. Integration with curves_of_form data
