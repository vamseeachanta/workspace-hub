## Mission

Research, assess, and implement calculation frameworks for artificial lift systems — covering sucker rod pumping (dynamometry), electric submersible pumps (ESP), gas lift, and progressive cavity pumps (PCP) — suitable for fast-running engineering assessment and diagnostic analysis.

## What / Why

Artificial lift enables production from wells that cannot flow naturally. Existing `digitalmodel/src/digitalmodel/marine_ops/artificial_lift/dynacard/` has dynamometer card analysis modules. This study will build a comprehensive literature base:

- **Sucker rod pumping** — wave equation (Gibbs), dynamometer card interpretation, pump fillage, rod string design (API 11L)
- **Dynamometer diagnostics** — surface and downhole card shapes, pattern recognition, efficiency calculation
- **ESP design** — pump curve selection, motor sizing, cable design, gas handling, variable speed operation
- **Gas lift** — injection pressure optimisation, valve spacing (API RP 11V6), stability analysis, continuous vs intermittent
- **PCP design** — elastomer selection, speed-flow relationship, torque requirements, rod string loading
- **Artificial lift selection** — screening criteria, economic comparison, well lifecycle considerations
