## Mission

Research, assess, and implement calculation frameworks for hydrodynamics and wave mechanics — covering wave spectra, wave loading (Morison equation, diffraction), RAO analysis, ship motions, and hull resistance — suitable for fast-running engineering assessment.

## What / Why

Hydrodynamics is foundational to all marine/offshore engineering. Existing `digitalmodel/src/digitalmodel/hydrodynamics/` has modules for wave spectra, coefficient database, OCIMF loading, RAO analysis, diffraction, hull library, and passing ship effects. This study will:

- **Wave spectra** — JONSWAP, Pierson-Moskowitz, Bretschneider, Ochi-Hubble; spectral moments, Hs/Tp relationships
- **Wave loading** — Morison equation (drag + inertia), diffraction theory (MacCamy-Fuchs), stream function wave theory
- **RAO and vessel motions** — 6-DOF response, strip theory basics, spectral analysis of motions
- **Hull resistance** — Holtrop-Mennen, ITTC 1957, form factor methods, added resistance in waves
- **OCIMF loading** — wind/current force coefficients for tankers and FPSOs
- **Current loading** — steady current forces on cylinders, groups, interference

Ground existing implementations in classical references and validate.
