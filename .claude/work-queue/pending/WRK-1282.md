## Mission

Research and document drive-off analysis methodologies — the transient event where
a DP vessel loses station-keeping and accelerates under propeller thrust before
emergency shutdown or manual intervention.

## What

- Literature survey of drive-off scenarios (API RP 16Q, DNV-OS-E101, OTC papers)
- Identify key physics: thrust ramp-up, drag buildup, drift trajectory
- Map to existing propeller-rudder models (WRK-1149: Söding + AD layered architecture)
- Document time-domain simulation approach for digitalmodel implementation

## Why

Drive-off is a critical safety scenario for DP vessels — understanding the transient
forces and vessel trajectory is essential for the WRK-1147 feature scope.
