## Mission
Extend the microgrid module with droop control simulation — frequency/voltage droop
response curves, governor modeling, and parallel generator load sharing analysis.

## What
- Module: `digitalmodel/src/digitalmodel/power/microgrid/droop_control.py`
- Models: governor droop (frequency-power), AVR droop (voltage-reactive), isochronous
- Simulation: steady-state load sharing for N parallel generators
- Analysis: droop setting optimization for target load split
- Standards: IEEE 1547 (DER interconnection), NFPA 110 (generator paralleling)

## Why
JD gap: "paralleling logic" — droop control is the fundamental mechanism for generator
paralleling and load sharing in data center and microgrid applications.
