## Mission

Prepare 2025 corporate taxes for AceEngineer Inc (C-Corp). File Form 1120 by April 15, 2026. File Texas franchise tax by May 15, 2026.

## What

- Collect all AceEngineer Inc financial records (revenue, expenses, payroll)
- Prepare Form 1120 (C-Corp return) — flat 21% federal rate
- File Texas franchise tax report + PIR by May 15, 2026
- Issue 1099-DIVs to shareholders if dividends were paid

## Why

C-Corp filing deadline April 15, 2026. Texas franchise tax PIR required by May 15 even if no tax owed.

## Documents Pending

- Revenue/income summary (P&L or bookkeeping export)
- 1099-NEC / 1099-MISC received from clients
- Business expense records / receipts
- Software & subscription costs
- Professional services (legal, accounting)
- Office / equipment purchases
- Business insurance
- W-2 issued to owners/employees
- W-3 (transmittal of W-2s)
- 941 quarterly payroll tax returns
- 940 (FUTA)
- EIN confirmation
- Prior year corporate tax return (2024)
- Vehicle / mileage log (if applicable)

## Acceptance Criteria

1. All AceEngineer financial documents collected in aceengineer-admin/taxes/2025/
2. Form 1120 prepared with corporate tax liability computed
3. Texas franchise tax report + PIR prepared
4. Filed or extension requested by April 15, 2026 (federal) and May 15, 2026 (TX)
