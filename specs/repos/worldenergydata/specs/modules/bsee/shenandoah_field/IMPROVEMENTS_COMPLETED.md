## üîç Critical Distinctions Now Emphasized

### Value Transfer vs. Value Creation
**Transfer:** $8.6B wealth moved from original partners to new partners  
**Creation:** Only $5.7B net value over 22 years (4-5% IRR)

### Individual Success vs. Societal Efficiency
**Individual:** New partners earn 37% IRR (private equity success)  
**Societal:** All-in IRR of 4-5% is below 15% hurdle rate (inefficient allocation)

### Asset Recycling Benefits
**FOR:** Rescued stranded resource, deployed technology majors avoided  
**AGAINST:** Same oil likely produced anyway; transaction costs are deadweight losses
