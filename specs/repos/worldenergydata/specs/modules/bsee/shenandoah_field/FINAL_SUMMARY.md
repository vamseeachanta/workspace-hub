## üìà Central Findings

### The Asset Lifecycle Story

**Total Capital Deployed:** $5.9 billion (2009-2026)

| Phase | Capital | Recovery | Outcome |
|-------|---------|----------|---------|
| **Original Partners** | $3.5B | $0 | -100% IRR (total loss) |
| **New Partners** | $3.0B | $11.6B expected | 37% IRR (high returns) |
| **All-In Project** | $5.9B | $11.6B expected | 4-5% IRR (sub-optimal) |

### The Societal Economics Question

**Does distressed asset recycling create value for society and the O&G industry?**

**Answer:** Mixed results.

**‚úÖ Value Transfer:** $8.6B wealth moved from old to new partners (efficient reallocation)  
**‚ùå Value Creation:** Only $5.7B net value over 22 years = 4-5% IRR (below 15% hurdle rate)

**The Paradox:** Individual success (PE earning 37%) coexists with collective underperformance (society earning 4-5%).
